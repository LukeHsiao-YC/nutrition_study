### 重點摘要
這篇文獻回顧旨在識別用於評估第二型糖尿病高風險或患者健康指導忠實度的工具。透過搜尋多個電子資料庫並篩選出15項研究，發現最常見的評估工具是檢查表（佔80%），然而其中僅有33.3%經過驗證。研究結果指出目前缺乏經過驗證且標準化的健康指導忠實度評估工具，這引發對研究發現有效性和可靠性的擔憂。因此，未來研究應著重於開發和驗證相關工具，以促進健康指導的標準化交付。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻揭示了目前在評估針對第二型糖尿病患者的健康指導忠實度時，缺乏經過驗證和標準化的評估工具，這對健康指導研究的有效性和可靠性構成了限制。未來的研究需要彌補此空白，開發並驗證這類工具。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Assessing the fidelity of health coaching for adults with or at risk of developing type 2 diabetes: A scoping review.
**Abstract:** The objective of this scoping review is to identify tools used to assess fidelity of health coaching for adults with or at risk of developing type 2 diabetes (T2D). We searched electronic databases, including MEDLINE, Embase, Emcare and PsycINFO. We included studies that reported data on health coaching components and tools used to measure fidelity of health coaching for adults with or at risk of developing T2D. We identified 4796 citations through our database and manual searches, with 15 studies meeting our inclusion criteria. The most common fidelity assessment tools were checklists (n = 12; 80%), either adapted from existing tools or newly developed, with only 33.3% being validated (n = 4). This review highlighted a lack of validated and standardized tools used to measure health coaching fidelity, raising concerns about validity and reliability of the findings. Future research is needed to support the development of validated tools and facilitate the standardization of health coaching delivery. Researchers and health care practitioners will gain valuable insights from our scoping review as it provides a comprehensive overview of health coaching fidelity tools. This is timely as health coaching is emerging as an effective strategy for supporting patients in adopting and sustaining healthy behaviours.
