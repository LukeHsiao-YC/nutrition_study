### 重點摘要
全球肥胖盛行率上升，而肥胖是心血管疾病（CVD）的主要危險因子。本研究開發並驗證了一個專門用於肥胖個體CVD分類的模型。研究方法包括篩選危險因子（年齡、高血壓、糖尿病、METS-VF、血清肌酐酸、血尿素氮）並建立預測列線圖，透過AUC、校準曲線和決策曲線分析評估其效能。該模型在內部驗證中AUC為0.799，外部驗證中AUC為0.821，顯示出良好的臨床實用性，為肥胖族群提供了一個經過優化且驗證的CVD分類工具。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
本研究填補了為肥胖個體量身打造、專門分類心血管疾病風險模型的空白，提供了一個針對此特定高風險族群優化的實用工具。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** A CVD Classification Model for Individuals With Obesity: Multi-Ethnic Validation Based on Multiple Metabolic Indicators.
**Abstract:** The global rise in obesity, a major risk factor for cardiovascular disease (CVD), contributes substantially to the disease burden. We developed and validated a model optimized for classifying prevalent CVD in individuals with obesity. In the training cohort, risk factors were screened by univariate and LASSO regression and then incorporated into a multivariate logistic model to establish the classifier. Based on this model, a nomogram was constructed. The area under the curve (AUC), calibration curve, and decision curve analysis (DCA) were used to evaluate model performance. Sensitivity was analyzed using a unified BMI standard in the Korea cohort. A web-based dynamic nomogram was also implemented (https://min115.shinyapps.io/CVDrisk/). The final nomogram incorporated age, hypertension, diabetes, metabolic score for visceral Fat (METS-VF), serum creatinine, and blood urea nitrogen. In internal validation, the model achieved an AUC of 0.799, with a Hosmer-Lemeshow p value of 0.823. In external validation, the AUC was 0.821, with a Hosmer-Lemeshow p value of 0.083. In both the internal and external validation sets, the model demonstrated good clinical utility. This study developed and validated a nomogram for the classification of prevalent CVD in individuals with obesity, providing a validated model optimized for the population with obesity.
