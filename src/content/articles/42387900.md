### 重點摘要
這項描述性世代研究旨在探討荷蘭腸衰竭中心中，因醫學上無法解釋的腸道餵食不耐受而接受居家全靜脈營養（HPN）的患者的臨床特徵、醫療消耗及治療狀況。研究分析了78名成年患者，發現大多數為女性（91%），80%在45歲或以下開始出現腸胃道症狀，且63%使用鴉片類藥物。結果顯示，83%的患者未能成功停用全靜脈營養，且照顧這些患者對醫師造成了極大負擔。研究建議在開始為不明原因腸道不耐受患者進行全靜脈營養治療前，應謹慎並尋求專家中心的評估。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這項研究填補了對醫學上無法解釋腸道餵食不耐受而接受全靜脈營養治療的患者缺乏系統性臨床描述的空白，明確指出這群患者的特徵及對醫療體系的負擔。然而，它是一項描述性研究，並未探討導致這種不耐受的潛在機制，也未提供有效的干預策略或斷奶方法，這將是未來研究的限制與方向。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Characterization of patients with medically unexplained intolerance to enteral feeding treated with home parenteral nutrition: A descriptive cohort study.
**Abstract:** Dutch intestinal failure centers are increasingly confronted with patients started on total parenteral nutrition because of reported intolerance to enteral feeding without meeting clinical criteria for intestinal failure. This study aimed to describe clinical characteristics, medical consumption, and treatment status of patients with medically unexplained intolerance to enteral feeding treated with home parenteral nutrition. We performed a descriptive cohort study in three intestinal failure expert centers in the Netherlands. We identified adult patients on total parenteral nutrition without a medical explanation for intolerance to enteral feeding via an electronic medical record search. We investigated patient characteristics, diagnostic procedures, and treatments these individuals had undergone. We also evaluated the impact of caring for these patients on physicians, using a questionnaire. 78 patients on total parenteral nutrition and with medically unexplained intolerance to enteral feeding were analyzed. Most patients were female (91%), 80% were ≤45 years at the start of gastrointestinal complaints, 63% were on opioids, and in 83% discontinuation of total parenteral nutrition was not achieved. The impact on treating physicians was rated as at least very burdensome. Treating patients with medically unexplained intolerance to enteral feeding with total parenteral nutrition is challenging for involved professionals in Dutch intestinal failure expert centers. This mainly concerns relatively young, female patients, most using opioids, and weaning off total parenteral nutrition is not successful in most cases. We suggest using caution when initiating total parenteral nutrition in patients with unexplained enteral intolerance before consultation with and/or assessment at an expert center.
