### 重點摘要
這項回溯性研究旨在探討在具末端空腸造口及失功能結腸的短腸症患者中，術前經皮內視鏡結腸造口（PEC）合併腸糜再灌注對造口逆轉術後長期預後（特別是液體獨立性）的影響。研究納入71名患者，其中57名接受PEC治療，14名接受常規照護，結果顯示PEC治療獨立預測造口逆轉術後成功脫離靜脈輸液。此外，PEC組患者術前所需的液體與電解質補充顯著減少，且在PEC組中，剩餘腸道長度 ≥100 公分是液體獨立的唯一預測因子。研究結論認為，術前PEC合併腸糜再灌注是安全的，並可能改善這些患者術後的液體獨立性，尤其對於剩餘腸道長度足夠的患者。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這項研究填補了在具有末端空腸造口和失功能結腸的短腸症患者中，術前經皮內視鏡結腸造口（PEC）合併腸糜再灌注對造口逆轉術後長期預後（特別是液體獨立性）影響尚不明確的研究空白。然而，由於本研究為回溯性設計，且對照組樣本量較小，未來的研究應考慮採用前瞻性設計以進一步確認其治療效益。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Association of preoperative chyme reinfusion via percutaneous endoscopic colostomy with post-reversal Fluid independence in short bowel syndrome patients with an end jejunostomy and an intact but defunctionalized colon.
**Abstract:** In patients with short bowel syndrome (SBS) with an end jejunostomy and an intact but defunctionalized colon, whether preoperative chyme reinfusion via percutaneous endoscopic colostomy (PEC) improves long-term outcomes after stoma reversal remains unclear. This retrospective study included 71 SBS patients with an end jejunostomy and an intact colon who underwent stoma reversal (2019-2024). Fifty-seven received PEC with chyme reinfusion; 14 received conventional care. The primary outcome was weaning from intravenous fluid replacement after stoma reversal. Median follow-up was 19 months. PEC placement independently predicted successful fluid weaning after reversal (HR = 1.97; P = 0.026). Among PEC patients, residual bowel length ≥100 cm was the only predictor (HR = 1.99; P = 0.025). During preoperative treatment, PEC patients required significantly less fluid replacement (difference = 851.6 mL, P < 0.001) and electrolyte supplementation. Post-reversal PN weaning rates were comparably high (97.2% overall). PEC-related complications occurred in 4 patients (7.0%), all minor. Preoperative PEC with chyme reinfusion appears safe and may be associated with improved post-reversal fluid independence in SBS patients with an end jejunostomy and an intact but defunctionalized colon, particularly those with residual bowel length ≥100 cm.
