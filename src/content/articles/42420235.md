### 重點摘要
肥胖患者常因體重汙名化而自尊低下，本研究旨在根據基線自尊程度，探討肥胖治療中生活型態介入選擇團體或個人訪視的建議。一項為期24個月、包含1220名農村初級照護患者的隨機對照試驗，比較了團體與個人治療的體重減輕效果。研究結果顯示，團體治療的體重減輕效果優於個人治療。特別是，若患者的基線體重相關自尊分數高於60分，可能從團體介入中獲益更多。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
本研究填補了在肥胖行為介入中，依據患者基線自尊程度選擇團體或個人治療的決策空白。它明確指出體重相關自尊分數高於特定閾值（60分）的患者可能從團體介入中獲益更多，為肥胖治療提供更個人化的介入策略建議。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Weight-Related Self-Esteem, Group Behavioral Therapy, and Weight Loss.
**Abstract:** Patients with obesity report experiencing weight stigma resulting in poor self-esteem. Based on baseline self-esteem, this paper explores recommendations for group versus individual visits for lifestyle components of comprehensive obesity treatment in primary care. Participants (n = 1220) were rural primary care patients in a 24-month cluster randomized controlled trial that demonstrated superior weight loss with group versus individual treatment. Baseline weight-related self-esteem scores (0-100), as measured by the Impact of Weight on Quality of Life-Lite (IWQOL-L), were used. A Bayesian normal dynamic linear model with a changepoint parameter estimates the relationship between self-esteem and weight loss across group versus individual treatment at the 24-month primary endpoint. The posterior probability that patients receiving group lost more weight than those receiving individual visits remained  People may benefit more from group than individual intervention if their self-esteem is above an IWQOL-L score of 60 when starting an intensive behavioral intervention for weight loss.
