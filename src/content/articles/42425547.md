### 重點摘要
本研究旨在通過制定全面的直接入院指南，在不犧牲患者安全的前提下，安全地增加非預約住院的直接入院比例，以緩解急診室的擁擠狀況。研究團隊在一間兒童醫院實施了集中化轉運中心、主治醫師監督及涵蓋所有診斷、年齡與專科的直接入院指南，並追蹤其對直接入院比例、急診室停留時間及患者安全指標的影響。結果顯示，直接入院比例從干預前的12%安全提升至18%，急診室住院患者的平均停留時間從335分鐘顯著縮短至288分鐘，且直接入院患者首次臨床評估時間也大幅提前。這表明廣泛包容的直接入院指南能有效提高直接入院比例並減輕急診室負擔，同時維持患者安全。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻填補了在兒科醫院環境中，透過廣泛包容的指南安全增加直接入院比例的可行性與效益的研究空白。未來的研究限制在於，此研究是在單一兒童醫院進行，其發現可能需要進一步探索如何在其他醫療系統中進行調整與應用。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Increasing Direct Admissions Safely with an Inclusive Guideline.
**Abstract:** Direct admission (DA) to the hospital bypasses the receiving hospital's emergency department (ED) and has been shown to offset ED overload without compromising patient safety. We aimed to increase the proportion of unscheduled admissions admitted directly. We tracked DA to our children's hospital between January 1, 2021, and October 31, 2025. Interventions included centralization of unscheduled admissions within our Transfer Center, establishment of hospitalist attending oversight, and development of a DA guideline inclusive of all diagnoses, ages, and specialties. Interventions went live May 2023. Our primary outcome was the proportion of unscheduled admissions admitted directly, and our secondary outcome was ED length of stay (LOS) for patients admitted through the ED. Balancing measures included escalations of care within 6 hours, time to first clinical assessment, and hospitalization under 12 hours. We used descriptive statistics and statistical process control to assess the impact of our interventions. DA increased from 12% preintervention to 18% postintervention without increasing intensive care transfers. During the same period, there was a sustained shift in ED LOS for admitted patients from 335 to 288 minutes. The median time to initial clinical assessment was 11 minutes faster for DA compared with ED admission (DA: 3 minutes, IQR 0-9 minutes vs ED: 14 minutes, IQR 7-30 minutes; P < .001). ED admissions were more likely to be hospitalized less than 12 hours (DA: 3.0% vs ED: 5.0%, P < .001). A broadly inclusive DA guideline safely increased DA. Future work may explore adaptation within other health systems.
