### 重點摘要
近期聯邦預防性醫療指引的變動，已影響民眾對預防保健的看法。本研究透過全國568名基層醫療臨床醫師的調查，評估這些變動對其執業與病患的影響。結果顯示，患者對疫苗和預防服務的猶豫度增加，例如64%對疫苗指南感到困惑，46%的家長對兒童疫苗產生新猶豫，且臨床醫師需花費更多時間解釋醫學證據，病患也更常尋求非醫療資訊，此現象在農村地區尤其明顯。研究結論指出，這些指引變動正使基層醫療難以提供循證預防服務，可能導致可預防疾病的增加，特別是在農村地區。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻填補了近期聯邦預防性醫療指引變動對基層醫療執業和患者預防保健行為影響的即時評估空白，特別突顯了政策變化如何直接導致患者對疫苗和預防服務的困惑與猶豫。然而，本研究為跨領域問卷調查，主要依賴臨床醫師的自我報告，並未直接收集患者數據或追蹤長期健康結果，因此無法確立政策變動與疾病發生率之間的直接因果關係，亦未深入探討具體政策細節。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** The effect of recent changes in federal preventive guidance on patients and primary care practice.
**Abstract:** Recent federal policy discussions and guidance have altered perceptions of preventive care. This study assesses the effect of recent changes in federal preventive guidance on primary care practice and patients. A national crowd-sourced survey of primary care clinicians, conducted December 4 to 11, 2025. Among 568 respondents from 47 states, over sampled for family medicine and with demographic characteristics similar to national primary care characteristics in terms of rurality, size, ownership, and percent in direct primary care, clinicians report that patients are increasingly hesitant about vaccines and preventive services. For example: 64% report patients are confused as to what vaccination guidelines they should follow; 46% report parents are expressing new hesitancy about childhood vaccinations; 36% report patients are declining preventive services they previously accepted. Most responding clinicians (58%) report spending more visit time explaining the evidence behind clinical recommendations, and 61% report patients more frequently are seeking information from non-medical sources. Findings differ by location, with 58% of rural clinicians, vs. 42% of non-rural clinicians reporting new hesitancy about childhood vaccinations. Recent changes in Federal guidance are making it more difficult to provide evidence-based preventive services on the frontlines of primary care, with worrisome potential for increases in preventable diseases, particularly in rural areas.
