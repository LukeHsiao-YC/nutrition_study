### 重點摘要
本研究旨在評估《刺胳針糖尿病與內分泌學》委員會提出的臨床肥胖新定義的預後價值，因其先前不明確。研究分析了中國、英國及美國三個大型世代研究（CHARLS、ELSA、NHANES）的數據，並使用Cox比例風險模型評估臨床肥胖與心血管事件及死亡風險的關聯。結果顯示，臨床肥胖的盛行率因隊列、年齡和性別而異，且與無功能障礙的非肥胖者相比，臨床肥胖和有功能障礙的非肥胖者均具有較高的心血管事件風險。此外，與臨床前肥胖相比，臨床肥胖顯著提高了多個隊列中的心血管事件和死亡風險，突顯了其在風險分層中的重要性。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻填補了《刺胳針糖尿病與內分泌學》委員會提出的臨床肥胖新定義在預後價值方面的研究空白，透過多個世代研究證實其與心血管事件及全因死亡風險的關聯，有助於未來進行風險分層。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** The Clinical Outcomes of Preclinical and Clinical Obesity Across Multiple Cohorts.
**Abstract:** The Lancet Diabetes & Endocrinology Commission proposed a new definition for clinical obesity. However, its prognostic value remains unclear. Clinical obesity was defined using the Commission's criteria in the China Health and Retirement Longitudinal Study (CHARLS), the English Longitudinal Study of Aging (ELSA), and the National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey (NHANES). Prevalence estimation was conducted across four categories based on obesity and organ dysfunction status. Cox proportional hazards models were used to assess associations with clinical outcomes. The prevalence of clinical obesity was 2.50% (CHARLS), 6.18% (ELSA), and 9.08% (NHANES), respectively. Overall, older adults and men had lower prevalence, while older men in NHANES showed higher prevalence. Compared with non-obesity without dysfunctions, clinical obesity and non-obesity with dysfunctions showed higher risks of cardiovascular outcomes. Compared with preclinical obesity, clinical obesity elevated cardiovascular risk in CHARLS (HR = 2.49, 95% CI: 1.32-4.70) and ELSA (HR = 1.80, 95% CI: 1.02-3.17) and increased all-cause (HR = 1.88, 95% CI: 1.23-2.87) and cardiovascular (HR = 3.11, 95% CI: 1.49-6.50) mortality risk in NHANES. The prevalence of clinical obesity varied by age, sex, and country. Overall, the prevalence was lower among older adults and men. Clinical obesity was associated with increased cardiovascular event risk across cohorts and increased all-cause mortality risk in NHANES, highlighting its value for risk stratification.
