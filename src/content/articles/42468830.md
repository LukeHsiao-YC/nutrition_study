### 重點摘要
本研究旨在探討嬰兒出生後1至8.5個月期間人乳攝取量（HM）的變化軌跡及其預測因子，以支持最佳餵養實踐。這項多中心隊列研究分析了來自孟加拉、巴西、甘比亞和丹麥的665對健康母嬰，透過測量人乳攝取量並使用縱向混合效應模型進行分析。結果顯示人乳攝取量隨時間呈現顯著的非線性變化，且母親身體質量指數、嬰兒性別、6個月純母乳餵養、嬰兒年齡別體重Z分數及哺乳頻率均能預測人乳攝取量。特別是純母乳餵養、嬰兒年齡別體重Z分數和哺乳頻率塑形了人乳攝取量的變化軌跡，填補了多元文化背景下人乳攝取量數據的不足。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
過去在不同族群中關於人乳攝取量模式與決定因素的數據有限，本研究透過多中心隊列研究，成功補足了孟加拉、巴西、甘比亞及丹麥等多元文化背景下，從出生後1至8.5個月人乳攝取量的詳細軌跡及預測因子數據。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Trajectory and predictors of human milk intake from 1 to 8.5 months of lactation: Findings from the multicenter MILQ cohort study.
**Abstract:** Understanding patterns and determinants of human milk (HM) intake in early infancy is important for supporting optimal practices; however, data are limited across diverse populations. To describe the trajectory of HM intake (g/day) from 1-8.5 months postpartum and to identify sociodemographic, maternal, infant, and breastfeeding-related predictors of HM intake. This multicenter cohort analysis from the Mothers, Infants and Lactation Quality study included 665 healthy mother-infant dyads assessed at 1-3.49 (M1), 3.5-5.99 (M2), and 6-8.5 (M3) months postpartum. HM intake was assessed at M1-M3 using the dose-to-mother technique in Bangladesh, Brazil, and The Gambia, and 24-hour test-weighing in Denmark. Longitudinal mixed-effects models with linear and quadratic terms for postpartum time were used to examine HM intake trajectories and identify predictors. Interaction models were adjusted for maternal body mass index (BMI), infant sex, exclusive breastfeeding (EBF) for 6 months, and infant weight-for-age (WAZ) z-score. An additional model included breastfeeding frequency for three sites.
