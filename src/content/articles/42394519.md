### 重點摘要
這項傘狀回顧旨在識別學齡兒童與青少年學校飲食與營養教育（SBFNE）中最有效且具一致性的教育與計畫組成要素。研究人員系統性地篩選了44篇文獻回顧與統合分析，涵蓋了1,651項原始研究。結果共辨識出20個不同的教育與計畫組成要素，其中部分要素與正向飲食行為改變呈現高度相關。本研究提供的構成要素框架為SBFNE的計畫設計、評估及政策制定提供了實用且可轉移的基礎。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
本研究透過將分析重點從個別介入措施轉移至跨領域的組成要素，填補了過去研究在SBFNE有效成分綜合性理解上的空白。它明確了哪些元素最能有效支持SBFNE，並提供了一個實用且可轉移的框架，以指導未來的計畫設計、評估及政策決策。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Educational and Programmatic Components of Effective School-Based Food and Nutrition Education: An Umbrella Review.
**Abstract:** To identify and synthesize the educational and programmatic components most consistently associated with effective school-based food and nutrition education (SBFNE) for children and adolescents. An umbrella review was conducted in accordance with PRISMA 2020 guidelines. We systematically searched CLEO, EDUCAT+, PubMed, and Google Scholar through December 2022 for eligible literature reviews and meta-analyses. Included reviews were assessed for methodological quality and synthesized at the component level using qualitative and quantitative approaches. Reviews examined SBFNE interventions delivered primarily during regular school hours in primary and secondary education settings worldwide. Forty-four literature reviews synthesizing evidence from 1,651 primary studies (1,115 unique) were included. Across reviews, 20 distinct educational and programmatic components were identified and standardized. The resulting component framework provides a practical, transferable foundation to inform program design, evaluation, and policy decision-making and to strengthen the equity, coherence, and effectiveness of SBFNE initiatives.
