### 重點摘要
母乳對嬰兒營養至關重要，但患有乳糜胸和嚴重長鏈脂肪酸去氫酶缺乏症的嬰兒常需改用脫脂母乳，這可能影響其生長發育。本研究旨在評估不同脫脂方法（包括離心、電動奶油分離器和重力分離）對母乳微量營養素成分的影響。結果顯示，所有脫脂方法均顯著降低了維生素A、E、葉酸、鋅和碘的含量，其中冷藏離心法在去除總三酸甘油酯方面最有效。這些發現可為食用脫脂母乳的特定疾病患者制定關鍵微量營養素的強化與補充指南。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
本研究填補了不同脫脂方法對母乳中關鍵微量營養素影響程度的知識空白，為臨床實踐提供了具體的營養成分數據。然而，研究限制可能包括未評估不同人群母乳成分的差異，且僅分析營養成分，缺乏長期食用脫脂母乳對嬰兒實際生長發育的臨床追蹤數據。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Comparison of methods for defatted human milk and nutrient composition: An experimental study.
**Abstract:** Human milk is considered the gold standard for infant nutrition; however, it is often discontinued in chylothorax and severe very long-chain acyl-CoA dehydrogenase (VLCAD) deficiency. As an alternative to complete human milk restriction, defatted human milk may be fed, though it may not fully support optimal infant growth and development. This study aimed to assess the micronutrient profile of defatted human milk. Human milk was obtained from UCSD Human Milk Research Biorepository (San Diego, CA). Thirty samples representing 10 independent expressions in the morning, afternoon, and evening were pooled for analysis. Fat removal was performed by centrifugation at refrigerated and room temperatures, electric cream separator, and gravity separation. Analyses of macronutrients, micronutrients, fat-soluble vitamins, and water-soluble vitamins were conducted in triplicate. All defatting methods decreased vitamins A and E by >80%. Folate decreased most by nonrefrigerated centrifugation (52.3, SD < 0.01%) and least by cream separator (22.8, SD < 0.01%). Zinc decreased most by both nonrefrigerated centrifugation (43.5 ± 0.06%) and cream separator (43.5 ± 0.12%), and least by refrigerated centrifugation (17.7 ± 0.10%). Iodine decreased most by refrigerated centrifugation (40.0, SD < 0.01%) and least by cream separator (31.2, SD < 0.01%). Total triglycerides decreased most by refrigerated centrifugation (87.0 ± 0.03%). All defatting methods decreased amounts of vitamin A, vitamin E, folate, zinc, and iodine. Refrigerated centrifuge was most effective in removing total triglycerides. These results can inform fortification and supplementation guidelines of key micronutrients for patients with chylothorax and VLCAD deficiency consuming defatted human milk.
