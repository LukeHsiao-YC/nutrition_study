### 重點摘要
本研究旨在探討焦葡萄酸鈉（SP）在高碳水化合物飲食（HCD）引起的魚類代謝紊亂中的作用及其機制。研究團隊利用斑馬魚、尼羅羅非魚進行體內實驗，並輔以斑馬魚肝細胞進行體外模型研究。結果顯示，補充焦葡萄酸鈉能顯著減少兩種魚類的肝臟脂質堆積、全身脂肪含量及血清三酸甘油酯水平，並提升肝臟磷脂含量與超氧化物歧化酶活性。機制研究揭示，焦葡萄酸鈉透過下調cPLA2-花生四烯酸-PPARγ通路來緩解高葡萄糖誘導的代謝紊亂，為優化魚類高碳水化合物飲食的利用提供了新策略。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻填補了焦葡萄酸在高碳水化合物飲食下，其在魚類脂質代謝調節中的作用及其具體分子機制尚不明確的研究空白，並為未來優化魚類飼料配方及解決高碳水化合物飲食引起的代謝問題提供了理論基礎和新見解。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Sodium Pyruvate Alleviates Lipid Deposition in Fish Caused by A High-Carbohydrate Diet via Suppression of the cPLA2-Arachidonic Acid-PPARγ Axis.
**Abstract:** Pyruvate is a critical node linking glycolysis and lipid metabolism. However, its regulatory role and mechanism in fish fed high-carbohydrate diets (HCD) remain obscure. In this study, we determined the role of sodium pyruvate (SP) in metabolic disorders induced by HCD and elucidated its underlying mechanisms using in vivo and in vitro models. All-male 4-month-old AB strain zebrafish (Danio rerio) (0.356 ± 0.02 g) were fed a control diet, HCD, or HCD supplemented with 2% or 4% SP for 4 wk (3 tanks/treatment, 30 fish/tank). Juvenile all-male 3-month-old Nile tilapia (Oreochromis niloticus) (4.00 ± 0.19 g) were fed a control diet, HCD, or HCD with 1% or 2% SP for 8 wk (3 tanks/treatment, 30 fish/tank). Zebrafish liver cell line (ZFL) were treated with 10 mM glucose, 25 mM glucose, or 25 mM glucose plus 4 or 8 mM SP for 24 h (n = 3). Data were analyzed by one-way ANOVA followed by Dunnett's post-hoc test. SP reduced hepatic lipid accumulation in both species. In zebrafish, 4% SP reduced whole-body lipid content by 36.7% and hepatic TG by 71.5% compared with HCD (P < 0.01). In Nile tilapia, 2% SP lowered liver total lipid by 21.8%, serum TG by 50.7%, and hepatosomatic index by 13.2% (P < 0.05). SP increased hepatic phospholipid content by 52.2% and hepatic superoxide dismutase activity by 21.0% (P < 0.05). In zebrafish liver cells, 8 mM SP reduced intracellular triglyceride by 59.2% compared with high-glucose treatment (P < 0.001).
