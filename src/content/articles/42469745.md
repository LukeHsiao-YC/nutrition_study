### 重點摘要
本研究旨在評估概念構圖教學對公共衛生學生的布魯姆認知水平和學習動機的影響。研究採用單組前測後測準實驗設計，對62名大學生進行為期15週的概念構圖教學介入。結果顯示，介入後學生在知識、理解、分析、綜合、評鑑等多個認知領域及學習動機均有顯著提升。然而，應用層面未見顯著改善，提示需配合其他策略以培養實踐技能。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻證實了概念構圖教學能有效提升公共衛生學生的多個高階認知能力和學習動機。然而，其單組設計限制了結果的普適性，且未能有效提升「應用」層面的認知能力，這指出未來的研究需要探索輔助策略來培養學生的實踐技能，並進行對照組研究以進一步確認這些初步發現。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** The effect of concept mapping-based instruction on the enhancement of bloom's cognitive levels and academic motivation among students of the school of public health.
**Abstract:** Concept mapping is an active learning strategy that may promote meaningful learning and higher-order cognitive development. This study aimed to evaluate the effect of concept mapping-based instruction on Bloom's cognitive levels and academic motivation among undergraduate public health students. This one-group pretest-posttest quasi-experimental study was conducted among 62 undergraduate students at Kerman University of Medical Sciences, Iran, in 2025. Educational content in Lessons was delivered using concept mapping over 15 weeks. Bloom's cognitive domains and academic motivation were assessed using researcher-developed questionnaire. Paired t-test and Pearson's correlation were used for analysis. Significant positive correlations were observed between all Bloom's domains and academic motivation (P < 0.001). Following the intervention, significant improvements were found in Knowledge (P = 0.005), Comprehension (P < 0.001), Analysis (P = 0.008), Synthesis (P = 0.001), Evaluation (P < 0.001), and Academic Motivation (P = 0.042). However, the Application domain did not show a significant increase (P = 0.077). Concept mapping-based instruction significantly enhanced multiple Bloom's cognitive levels and academic motivation among public health students. The lack of improvement in the application domain suggests that developing practical skills may require complementary strategies. Further controlled studies are needed to confirm these preliminary findings.
