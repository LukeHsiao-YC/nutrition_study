### 重點摘要
儘管性健康對整體福祉至關重要，但在心理治療中卻常被忽視，本研究旨在探討心理治療師和病人相關的評估障礙。這項針對德國220名心理治療師的混合方法研究，分析了治療師特徵與性健康評估頻率的關聯，並透過主題分析探討病人相關障礙。結果顯示，居住在城市地區、採精神動力學取向、年齡較大、工作經驗較豐富且性知識較多的治療師，更常評估性健康。研究結論強調，基於這些已識別的障礙進行性教育是必要的，以提升治療師的能力和信心，進而顯著改善醫療照護。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
本研究透過混合方法，系統性地識別並探討了心理治療師在評估病人性健康時所面臨的治療師本身及病人相關的具體障礙，填補了此領域中對影響因素缺乏實證分析的空白。未來的研究可以進一步探討這些障礙的因果關係，並擴展到不同文化背景下的情境。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Barriers to the assessment of patients' sexuality and sexual health among psychotherapists: A mixed-methods study.
**Abstract:** Sexual health is a fundamental aspect of well-being, yet sexual problems remain highly prevalent and are rarely addressed in psychotherapy. This study examined psychotherapist- and patient-related factors preventing psychotherapists from addressing sexuality and sexual health in psychotherapy. In this cross-sectional study, anonymous data from 220 licensed psychotherapists and psychotherapists in training in Germany were collected online. Psychotherapist characteristics associated with more or less frequent sexual health assessment were analysed using t-tests, linear regression, and Pearson correlations. Responses to open-text questions regarding perceived patient-related barriers were analysed using thematic analysis. No significant gender differences were found among psychotherapists, t(216) = -0.44, d = -0.08, p = .329, in addressing sexual health. Psychotherapists working in urban areas (d = -0.31, p = .010) and those with psychodynamic orientation (d = 0.30, p = .015) addressed sexual health more frequently. Urban and psychodynamic therapists assess sexuality and sexual health more often. Various patient characteristics and interaction dynamics are perceived as sexual communication barriers by psychotherapists. Sexuality education based on the identified barriers is necessary to strengthen psychotherapists' competence and confidence in adressing sexuality in psychotherapy, which can improve health care significantly.
