### 重點摘要
傳統的動機式晤談 (MI) 訓練方式，如線上或實體研討會，雖普及但被證實未能有效提升臨床技能，反而可能導致學員的錯誤自信。若持續依賴此類教學，MI的推廣恐無法轉化為實際的臨床成效，此問題亦廣泛存在於行為健康介入領域。文章指出，唯有透過專家回饋與觀察實踐的指導，才能真正提高技能熟練度，並探討了專有控制、品質保證及訓練模式等核心議題，為未來訓練提出建議。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻明確指出當前行為健康介入訓練（特別是動機式晤談）中，過度依賴講授式教育的不足與風險，即無法有效提升臨床技能並可能造成假性自信。它填補了如何改進複雜行為介入訓練模式的討論空白，但同時也暗示了在實際實施專家回饋與品質保證的訓練體系方面，仍存在方法論和實踐上的挑戰。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Virga and the future of motivational interviewing: Issues in training counseling skills.
**Abstract:** Online and in-person workshops are most common in continuing professional education, but research shows that such didactic training alone is unlikely to increase clinical skill in motivational interviewing (MI) enough to benefit clients, though it can instill false confidence. If training continues to rely primarily on didactic education, the popularity and dissemination of MI may peak without improving clinical outcomes when those interested in learning MI believe they have done so. This risk is not unique to MI but may apply more generally with behavioral health interventions. What actually improves proficiency is akin to healthcare training in which learners receive expert feedback and coaching through observed practice until reaching a standard of competence. This article discusses three important issues in training complex behavioral interventions: (1) proprietary control, (2) quality assurance, and (3) training modalities. Implications and recommendations for future training are discussed.
