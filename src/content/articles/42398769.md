### 重點摘要
這篇系統性回顧與統合分析旨在評估乳脂肪球膜 (MFGM) 及其乳極性脂質 (MPLs) 對成年人心臟代謝疾病風險標記物與認知功能的影響。研究方法是篩選並統合13項隨機對照試驗的數據，比較MFGM/MPL產品與低含量對照組的差異。結果顯示，攝取MFGM/MPL能顯著降低空腹總膽固醇、低密度脂蛋白膽固醇、載脂蛋白B及總膽固醇與高密度脂蛋白膽固醇比值。這表明MFGM/MPL對改善成年人的血脂狀況具有潛在益處。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這項研究透過綜合性的系統性回顧與統合分析，為乳脂肪球膜及其乳極性脂質在改善成年人血脂異常等心臟代謝風險因子方面的效益，提供了強有力的科學證據。然而，由於摘要中對認知功能結果的詳盡程度有限，未來研究或需更深入探討這些成分對認知健康的具體影響。

**Abstract:** Milk fat globule membrane (MFGM) and its associated milk polar lipids (MPLs) are bioactive components of dairy fat with emerging relevance for cardiometabolic and cognitive health. This systematic review and meta-analysis evaluated the effects of bovine-derived MFGM/MPL on fasting and postprandial cardiometabolic disease risk markers and cognitive function in disease-free adults, compared with isoenergetic dairy products or supplements low in these components. Searches of PubMed (MEDLINE), Embase, and Cochrane Central identified randomized controlled trials (RCTs) comparing MFGM/MPL-containing foods or supplements with isoenergetic dairy comparators low in these components. Risk of bias (RoB) was assessed using Cochrane RoB 2.0 tool. Random-effects meta-analyses were performed for outcomes reported in ≥ 3 studies, and certainty of evidence for critical outcomes was evaluated using the GRADE approach. Of 730 records screened, 13 studies (14 articles) met inclusion criteria (RoB: low to some concerns).
