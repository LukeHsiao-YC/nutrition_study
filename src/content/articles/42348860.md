### 重點摘要
鑑於網路霸凌在約旦青少年中日益盛行，本研究旨在透過敘事視角，深入探討青少年遭受網路霸凌的個人經驗與觀點。研究採用橫斷面設計，招募約旦中部與北部公立學校400名14-17歲學生，並對其中240名曾受網路霸凌的學生進行開放式問卷調查，透過主題分析歸納出網路霸凌的影響、克服其後果的挑戰，以及影響其嚴重性的因素三大主題。這些發現為創建更安全的網路環境和透過適當干預措施減少網路霸凌的心理社會危害提供了寶貴見解。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
本研究透過敘事視角，填補了約旦青少年網路霸凌經驗在個人層面與觀點的理解空白，有助於深入掌握其心理社會影響，而非僅止於數據統計。然而，其橫斷面設計限制了對因果關係的探討，且未評估干預措施的成效。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Understanding Adolescents' Experiences of Cyberbullying in Jordan: Evidence From a Narrative Perspective.
**Abstract:** Based on its nature, cyberbullying is expected to be frequent and prevalent because of the continuity of technological advancement over time. This change results in more victims and is challenging to detect, negatively affecting their health. Given that adolescents in Jordan face a high rate of cyberbullying, it is essential to understand how this experience affects them. This study aims to explore individual experiences and perspectives on cyberbullying victimization among adolescents to intervene and reduce future incidences of cyberbullying. We based our analysis on a cross-sectional study investigating cyberbullying and its mental health consequences among 400 students aged 14-17 years from public schools in central and northern Jordan. These respondents were asked to answer 3 open-ended questions describing their experiences with cyberbullying if they experienced cybervictimization as either victims or bully-victims, resulting in 240 responses. Thematic analysis was then used to interpret patterns of shared meaning across participants' narratives related to cyberbullying experiences in cyberspace. A total of 3 key themes and several subthemes emerged from this study: (1) effects of cyberbullying, (2) challenges in overcoming its consequences, and (3) elements influencing the severity of cyberbullying experiences. The findings offer valuable insights for creating safer online environments and reducing cyberbullying's psychological and social harms through appropriate interventions.
