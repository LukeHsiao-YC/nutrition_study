### 重點摘要
這項研究旨在探討R-spondin 3 (RSPO3) 蛋白表達水平對體脂和血脂的性別分層影響及其潛在的性激素介導作用。研究人員對近四萬名參與者進行了性別分層的基因組關聯研究 (GWAS) 和孟德爾隨機化 (MR) 分析，以評估RSPO3與代謝結果的關聯，並進一步探究了性激素的介導效應。結果顯示，在女性中，RSPO3蛋白水平與內臟脂肪組織、腰圍、臀圍、三酸甘油酯呈正相關，並與高密度脂蛋白膽固醇和載脂蛋白A呈負相關，且這些關聯存在明顯的性別差異，主要透過性激素結合球蛋白介導。研究結論指出，RSPO3有望成為調節女性體脂分佈和血脂的藥物靶點。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這項研究填補了RSPO3蛋白表達對體脂和血脂影響的性別分層差異，特別是其在女性中的顯著作用以及性激素（如性激素結合球蛋白）的介導機制。雖然研究指出RSPO3可能是女性體脂分佈和血脂調節的藥物靶點，但其臨床轉化仍需進一步的功能驗證與藥物開發研究。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** The Sex-Stratified Effects of RSPO3 Expression on Body Fat and Blood Lipids: An Omics Mendelian Randomization Study.
**Abstract:** This study examined sex-stratified effects of R-spondin 3 (RSPO3) expression levels on body fat and blood lipids and their potential mediation effects through sex hormones. We performed a sex-stratified genome-wide association study (GWAS) of RSPO3 protein (N = 18,529 men and 21,323 women). We used two-sample Mendelian randomization (MR) to examine associations of RSPO3 expression with the outcomes. Sex-stratified MR estimates were compared using the pairwise z score test and Cochran's Q test. We conducted two-step MR analyses to explore mediation effects through sex hormones. Sex-stratified GWAS identified nine loci associated with RSPO3 protein levels, including three novel signals and four female-stratified loci. In women, RSPO3 protein levels were associated with visceral adipose tissue (β = 0.22, 95% CI = 0.10 to 0.34), waist circumference (0.10, 0.05 to 0.15), hip circumference (0.10, 0.05 to 0.15), triglycerides (0.22, 0.18 to 0.26), high-density lipoprotein cholesterol (-0.17, -0.21 to -0.13), and apolipoprotein A (-0.11, -0.15 to -0.06). We also observed sex differences in these associations. Two-step MR showed that RSPO3 protein levels affected six outcomes via sex hormone-binding globulin in women, with little evidence in men. Our findings indicated that RSPO3 could be a drug target for regulating body fat distribution and blood lipids in women.
