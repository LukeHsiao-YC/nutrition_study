### 重點摘要
本研究開發了NutVal v5，一款旨在規劃和監測人道主義糧食援助營養內容和成本的試算表應用程式。該Excel工具能計算食物援助的巨量與微量營養素含量，並與14種不同人群（包括兒童、成人及被拘留者）的需求進行比較。此外，它允許輸入市場成本以計算現金轉移等值，並儲存和比較不同口糧。NutVal v5廣泛應用於人道組織，其版本5的增強功能特別適應日益普及的現金和代金券轉移模式，提升了糧食援助規劃和監測的效率與精確性。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇文獻填補了人道主義糧食援助領域中，缺乏一個全面且能同時處理實物食物與現金/代金券轉移的營養規劃與監測工具的空白。它提供了一種標準化的方法來評估不同人口群體的營養需求，並整合了成本分析，尤其應對了現金轉移日益增長的趨勢，確保援助的適用性和有效性。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Development of NutVal 5: A Spreadsheet Application for Planning and Monitoring Humanitarian Food Assistance.
**Abstract:** To develop a software application for planning and monitoring the nutritional content and cost of humanitarian food assistance, provided by food or cash transfers. Development of software. Humanitarian emergencies. Food rations or cash transfers provided to beneficiaries. NutVal v5 is an Excel application for calculation of the macro and micronutrient content of food assistance, and comparison with the requirements of 14 different population groups, including the population averaged requirements, child and adult sub-groups, and people in detention. The criteria for nutrient inclusion and the calculation of population group requirements are presented. Market costs can be entered for calculation of a cash-transfer equivalent value and different food rations can be saved and compared over time. The NutVal application has been widely used by humanitarian organisations to plan and monitor food transfers. The enhancements in version 5 should allow for its continued use in food assistance programmes where cash and voucher transfers are increasingly common.
