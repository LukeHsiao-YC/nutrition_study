### 重點摘要
這項研究評估了限制高脂、高糖、高鹽（HFSS）食品價格促銷或其溝通方式，對消費者購買行為的影響。透過一項涵蓋9,004名英國成年人的隨機對照試驗，在模擬線上超市中比較了三種介入模式（正常促銷、無溝通促銷、無促銷）。結果顯示，各組在購物籃的總能量、總成本、能量密度或HFSS食品比例上均無顯著差異，僅正常促銷組選購的商品數量略多。研究結論指出，在模擬環境下，限制HFSS食品的價格促銷或其溝通方式對購買行為影響不大，未來需在真實世界中進行更多價格干預研究。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
這篇研究填補了在模擬線上超市環境中，限制HFSS食品價格促銷對購買行為影響的研究空白，但並未發現顯著效果。其主要限制在於研究環境為模擬而非真實世界，因此結果可能無法直接推論至實際消費情境。未來的研究應著重於在真實世界的環境中，進一步探索價格導向的介入措施，以提供更堅實的政策建議。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** The impact of restricting price promotions on foods high in fat, sugar, and salt on purchasing behaviours: A randomised controlled trial in a simulated online supermarket.
**Abstract:** Price promotions influence food choices, and are disproportionately applied to foods high in fat, sugar, and salt (HFSS), consumption of which is a known contributor to poor diet and diet-related non-communicable diseases. This study evaluates the effectiveness of (1) restricting price promotions of HFSS products and (2) allowing such promotions but restricting communications regarding them, in changing purchasing behaviours. Between-subjects randomised controlled trial with three arms: (1) reduced price, labelled a price promotion ("Usual Practice"); (2) reduced price, not labelled a price promotion ("No Communication"); (3) standard price ("No Promotion"). Participants selected food and drinks for 2 days in a one-off shop, without restrictions on the number of items to purchase or the budget, at a simulated online supermarket. We measured total energy (kcal) in basket, total cost, total number of items, energy density (kcal/100g), and proportion of the basket that was HFSS. Simulated online supermarket. Adults representative of the UK population (n=9,004). There were no significant differences in energy in baskets between groups, after controlling for age, gender, household income, ethnicity, and deprivation. No significant differences were found between groups' baskets for total cost, energy density, or proportion HFSS. Further price-based interventions, especially in real-world settings, are needed to provide robust policy recommendations.
