### 重點摘要
兒童肥胖管理中肥胖藥物（ObM）的使用日益增加，但其影響因素尚不明確。本研究分析了來自12個POWER中心、共4628名6-18歲青少年的數據，比較了使用ObM與未使用者的臨床特徵以及各中心的處方模式。結果顯示，20%的患者使用了ObM，且主要為托吡酯和二甲雙胍，使用ObM的患者年齡更大、肥胖程度更嚴重且合併症更多。研究表明ObM處方與年齡、肥胖嚴重度及合併症顯著相關，強調了需要前瞻性研究來評估早期啟動治療以預防疾病進展的重要性。

### 研究縫隙 (Research Gap)
本研究填補了兒童體重管理計劃中肥胖藥物處方影響因素的知識空白，確認了年齡、肥胖嚴重度和合併症是關鍵的相關因素。然而，研究結果也指出了未來的研究限制，即目前缺乏關於肥胖藥物早期介入以預防疾病進展的前瞻性研究。

### 原文資訊 (Original English)
**Title:** Correlates of Obesity Medication Use in Paediatric Weight Management Programmes: A POWER Consortium Analysis.
**Abstract:** The national POWER registry of paediatric weight management programmes reported increased obesity medication (ObM) use in recent years. However, factors influencing ObM prescribing are not well understood. This study aimed to compare (1) clinical characteristics between youth prescribed ObMs and those who were not and (2) prescribing patterns among programmes. Data from youth aged 6-18 years seen at 12 POWER sites between 2018 and 2024 were analysed. Clinical and demographic variables were compared between ObM and non-ObM groups using Fisher's exact test and logistic regression, adjusting for site. Site-level prescribing patterns were assessed using χ ObMs were prescribed to 927 of 4628 (20%) patients. Topiramate (37%) and metformin (43%) were the most prescribed. ObM patients were older [12-15 years aOR (CI) = 1.7 (1.4, 2.0); 16-18 years aOR (CI) = 1.6 (1.3, 2.0) vs. 6-11 years], had more severe obesity [Class 2 aOR (CI) = 1.6 (1.3, 2.2); Class 3 aOR (CI) = 2.9 (2.3, 3.8) vs. Class 1] and had more obesity related co-morbidities, but demographic characteristics were not different. Higher prescribing programmes offered ObMs sooner and multiple ObMs more often. ObM prescribing was strongly associated with age, obesity severity and comorbidities, highlighting the need for prospective studies on earlier initiation to prevent disease progression.
